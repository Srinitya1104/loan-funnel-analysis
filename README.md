# 🏦 Banking Loan Application Funnel Analysis

## Problem Statement
A retail bank is losing significant loan business due to 
drop-offs at various stages of the application process.
This analysis identifies where and why applicants drop off
and quantifies the revenue impact.

## Tools Used
Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Dataset
5,000 simulated loan applicants with demographics,
credit scores, and funnel stage completion data.

## Key Findings
- Only **23.8%** of leads result in loan disbursement
- **Approval stage** has the highest drop-off (72.36% overall)
- **Document submission** loses 38.78% of applicants
- Estimated **₹190+ Crores** in potential loans lost to drop-offs
- Self-Employed applicants have lowest conversion rates

## Business Recommendations
1. Launch digital document upload portal to reduce docs drop-off
2. Introduce pre-screening credit check before full application
3. Create tailored loan products for self-employed segment
4. Target Tier 2 & Tier 3 cities — untapped market opportunity
